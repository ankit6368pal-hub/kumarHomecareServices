COMMON PROBLEMS

What We Fix

From heating failures to sparking issues — we handle all microwave problems safely and quickly.

Not Heating

Microwave runs but food stays cold? We replace faulty magnetrons, diodes, and capacitors to restore full heating power.

Sparking Inside

Sparking is dangerous and must be fixed immediately. We replace damaged waveguide covers, racks, and interior components.

Turntable Not Rotating

A stuck turntable leads to uneven cooking. We fix motor failures, coupler issues, and track problems on all brands.

Door Latch Broken

A broken door latch prevents the microwave from starting. We repair or replace latch assemblies and door switches.

Display Not Working

Blank or flickering display? We repair control boards, replace display modules, and restore all button functions.

Burning Smell

Burning smell indicates internal component damage. We safely inspect and replace faulty parts before they cause further damage.

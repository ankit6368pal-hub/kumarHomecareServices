COMMON PROBLEMS

What We Fix

From drum issues to drainage failures — we handle every washing machine problem.

Not Spinning

Drum not spinning or spinning too slowly? We fix motor failures, belt issues, and lid switch problems on all models.

Water Not Draining

Standing water after a wash cycle? We clear blocked drain pumps, hoses, and filters to restore proper drainage.

Door Won't Lock / Open

Stuck door locks and faulty interlocks are safety hazards. We repair or replace door mechanisms on all brands.

Noisy Drum / Vibration

Banging or excessive vibration during spin? We replace worn drum bearings, shock absorbers, and springs.

Not Filling with Water

Water inlet valve failures and blocked inlet hoses are common. We diagnose and fix low-pressure and solenoid issues.

Control Panel Error

Error codes on display, buttons not responding? We repair electronic control boards and reset washing machine programs.

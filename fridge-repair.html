COMMON PROBLEMS

What We Fix

From cooling failures to compressor issues — our technicians handle all refrigerator problems.

Not Cooling Properly

Fridge not maintaining temperature? We diagnose and fix cooling system failures, refrigerant leaks, and sensor issues.

Compressor Noise

Loud humming or rattling from your fridge? We repair or replace faulty compressors with original spare parts.

Ice Maker Not Working

Ice maker stopped producing ice? We fix clogged water inlets, faulty ice modules, and temperature control issues.

Water Leaking Inside

Water pooling at the bottom? We clear blocked drain pipes and fix defrost system malfunctions causing leaks.

Door Seal Broken

A worn door gasket wastes energy and ruins cooling. We replace door seals for all refrigerator models.

Thermostat Failure

Fridge too cold or not cold enough? We calibrate or replace faulty thermostats to restore perfect temperature.
